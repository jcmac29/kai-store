Writing and Content Prompts

Good writing prompts give ChatGPT context about your audience, tone, and objectives. Here are the most effective prompts for content creation.

Blog Post Outline Generator

Create a comprehensive blog post outline for the topic: [TOPIC]

Target audience: [AUDIENCE]
Word count goal: [NUMBER] words
Primary keyword: [KEYWORD]

Include:
- An attention-grabbing H1 title with the keyword
- 5-7 main sections (H2s) that cover the topic thoroughly
- 2-3 subsections (H3s) under each main section
- Key points to cover in each section
- Suggested statistics or data points to include
- A compelling introduction hook
- A strong conclusion with a call-to-action

Content Rewriter with Tone Control

Rewrite the following content in a [TONE] voice while:
- Maintaining the core message
- Making it more engaging for [TARGET AUDIENCE]
- Adding specific examples where appropriate
- Keeping it scannable with short paragraphs
- Preserving SEO value by keeping key phrases

Original content:
[PASTE CONTENT]

LinkedIn Post Creator

Write a LinkedIn post about [TOPIC] that:
- Opens with a hook that stops the scroll
- Shares a genuine insight or lesson learned
- Uses short paragraphs (1-2 sentences each)
- Includes a specific story or example
- Ends with a question to drive engagement
- Is between 150-200 words
- Avoids corporate jargon

My professional context: [YOUR ROLE/INDUSTRY]

Business Strategy Prompts

These prompts help you think through business decisions with AI as your strategic advisor. If you are interested in more business-focused prompts, check out our guide on best AI prompts for business.

SWOT Analysis Generator

Conduct a thorough SWOT analysis for [COMPANY/PROJECT]:

Business context: [BRIEF DESCRIPTION]
Industry: [INDUSTRY]
Target market: [MARKET]
Current stage: [STARTUP/GROWTH/MATURE]

For each category, provide:
- 5 specific, actionable items
- Supporting rationale
- Priority ranking (high/medium/low)

Then provide:
- 3 strategies to leverage strengths against threats
- 3 strategies to address weaknesses using opportunities

Competitive Analysis Framework

Analyze [COMPETITOR] as a competitor to [YOUR COMPANY]:

Evaluate these dimensions:
1. Product/service offerings - what do they do better/worse?
2. Pricing strategy - how do they position themselves?
3. Market positioning - who are they targeting?
4. Marketing channels - where are they most active?
5. Customer sentiment - what do reviews say?
6. Technology/innovation - what are their advantages?

Based on public information, identify:
- Their likely strategic priorities
- Gaps we could exploit
- Threats we should prepare for

Marketing and Copywriting Prompts

Effective marketing prompts focus on understanding the customer and crafting messages that resonate.

Create a detailed customer avatar for [PRODUCT/SERVICE]:

Product context: [BRIEF DESCRIPTION]
Price point: [AMOUNT]

Prompt Engineering Tips

Getting the best results from ChatGPT requires understanding how to structure your prompts effectively.

The CRAFT Framework

Use this framework for better prompts every time:

  • Context: Give background information
  • Role: Define who ChatGPT should be
  • Action: Specify exactly what you want
  • Format: Describe the output structure
  • Tone: Set the voice and style

Key Principles for Better Prompts

1. Be Specific, Not Vague

Instead of: "Write about marketing"
Use: "Write a 500-word guide on email marketing for B2B SaaS companies, focusing on welcome sequences that convert free trial users to paid customers."

2. Provide Examples

Show ChatGPT what good output looks like. Include a sample of the style, format, or approach you want.

3. Set Constraints

Limits actually improve output: word counts, format requirements, things to avoid, specific elements to include.

4. Iterate and Refine

Your first prompt rarely produces perfect results. Use follow-up prompts to refine: "Make this more conversational," "Add specific examples," "Shorten this to half the length."
