## How to Use This Pack

1. **Copy the prompt** - Select the prompt that matches your need
2. **Fill in the brackets** - Replace [placeholders] with your specific details
3. **Paste into ChatGPT/Claude** - Use your preferred AI assistant
4. **Iterate** - Ask follow-up questions to refine the output
5. **Implement** - Adapt the suggestions for your unique classroom

**Pro Tips:**
- Use the Lesson Plan Builder with the Differentiation Strategy Builder for fully inclusive lessons
- Pair the Parent Conference Preparation with Difficult Conversation Script for challenging meetings
