## How to Use This Pack

1. **Copy the prompt** - Select the prompt that matches your HR challenge
2. **Fill in the brackets** - Replace [placeholders] with your specific situation
3. **Paste into ChatGPT/Claude** - Use your preferred AI assistant
4. **Iterate** - Ask follow-up questions to customize the output
5. **Adapt** - Modify for your company culture and legal requirements

**Pro Tips:**
- Combine the Job Description Writer with Interview Question Bank for end-to-end hiring alignment
- Use the Performance Review Templates with Goal Setting Framework for integrated performance management
- Pair Stay Interviews with Employee Engagement Surveys for complete retention intelligence
- Always have legal counsel review policies and termination documentation

**Important:** AI-generated HR content should be reviewed by qualified HR professionals and legal counsel, especially for policies, legal documentation, and compliance-related materials.
