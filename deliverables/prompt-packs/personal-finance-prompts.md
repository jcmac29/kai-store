## How to Use This Pack

1. **Copy the prompt** - Select the prompt that matches your need
2. **Fill in the brackets** - Replace [placeholders] with your actual numbers
3. **Paste into ChatGPT/Claude** - Use your preferred AI assistant
4. **Verify the math** - Always double-check calculations with a calculator
5. **Take action** - Implement the plan step by step

**Important disclaimers:**
- AI provides education and analysis, not licensed financial advice
- Verify calculations independently before making decisions
- Consult professionals for complex tax situations
- Your situation is unique - use outputs as starting points
- Past performance and projections don't guarantee future results

**Pro tips:**
- Update prompts with new numbers quarterly
- Use outputs to prepare for meetings with financial advisors
- Chain prompts together (budgeting feeds into investing)
- Keep records of your plans to track progress
