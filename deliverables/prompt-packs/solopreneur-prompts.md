## Business Strategy (8 Prompts)

### 1. Solo Business Model Designer
```
Design a business model for my solo business.

What I do: [your skill/service/product]
Who I serve: [target customer]
My time available: [hours per week]
Income goal: [monthly target]
Location: [where you work from]

Design a model that:
1. Generates [income goal] per month
2. Uses no more than [hours] per week
3. Doesn't require employees
4. Can scale without me trading more time

Include:
- Revenue streams (primary + secondary)
- Pricing structure
- Client/customer volume needed
- Delivery model
- Tools to use
- What to outsource vs DIY
- Path from current state to this model
```

## How to Use This Pack

1. **Identify your struggle** - What's the biggest friction?
2. **Find the prompt** - Match it to your need
3. **Go deep** - Fill in every detail
4. **Iterate** - Ask follow-ups
5. **Implement** - Action beats planning

**Pro tip:** Revisit these quarterly. Your answers will evolve as your business does.
